Multimedia Formats Supported by HTML5

HTML5 supports the following multimedia formats:

  • Video Formats: MP4, WebM, Ogg
  • Audio Formats: MP3, WAV, Ogg

For best browser compatibility, always provide multiple formats for both video and audio elements.
