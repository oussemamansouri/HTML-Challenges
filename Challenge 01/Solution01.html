HTML Headings and Paragraphs

HTML Headings

HTML headings are used to define the titles and subtitles of a webpage. There are six heading levels, from <h1> to <h6>, with <h1> being the most important.

Using Paragraphs and Line Breaks

Paragraphs are used to create blocks of text on a webpage. A paragraph starts on a new line and is wrapped in <p> tags. You can also insert line breaks within a paragraph using the <br> tag.
